BBC Sport - Euro 2012

I spent the month of June assisting the BBC Sport journalists tackle the Euro's. Being football mad I jumped at the opportunity.

I created various graphics every day including the main promotional banner for each game to sit on the Sport homepage.

The majority of the work was creating infographics based on the match stats to illustrate the main points of the match reports.

Creative Cup

In 2010 I created a football league for creative agencies. The Creative Cup has gone on to raise just short of £6k for a number of charities. Without doubt the most rewarding project in my portfolio.

The likes of the BBC, Umbro and The Neighbourhood are just some of the teams currently playing in the league.

The Gate LAb

The Gate Lab project is a specialist community for script-writers and film-makers based around the concept that an idea is represented by a seed. You plant the seed and the community helps nurture and grow through the aid of feedback.
